#  NutriSmart

## Smart Nutrition & Healthy Lifestyle Platform

NutriSmart is an intelligent web platform designed to help users manage their nutrition, plan healthy meals, optimize food budgets, and reduce food waste.  
The platform combines health, technology, and sustainability to provide a personalized and modern user experience.


# Project Overview

NutriSmart helps users:

- Track their daily nutritional intake
- Plan meals according to personal goals
- Discover healthy recipes
- Manage food expenses intelligently
- Reduce food waste
- Monitor the environmental impact of food choices

The platform aims to promote a healthier and more sustainable lifestyle through smart digital solutions.
